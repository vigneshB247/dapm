Privacy Policy

DAPM SA complies strictly with the principles on data protection contained in the Swiss Data Protection Law (DSG CH) of June 19, 1992. DAPM SA respects the privacy (Article 13 of the Federal Constitution) of people whose data are being processed.

DAPM will use the information you provide for the purpose of wealth management supervision and consolidation. Your information will be held on our databases based on your data agreement confirmation and related contract.

You have the right to ask for a copy of the information we hold on you, for which we may charge a fee, and the right to ask us to correct any inaccuracies in that information.
If you do want to see a copy of your information, please contact the Data Protection Team by email: dataprotection@dapm.com or send in your request to the Data privacy officer, DAPM SA, Rue Pierre-Fatio, 15, 1204 Geneva, SWITZERLAND
